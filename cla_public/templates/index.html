Check if you can get legal aid

Legal aid can help pay for legal advice or family mediation.

This service will check that your problem is covered by legal aid.

  • If it looks like you can get legal aid, you may be able to get help over the phone or we’ll suggest that you speak to an adviser face to face.
  • If it looks like you can’t get legal aid, we’ll point you towards other organisations that offer legal advice, or help you find a solicitor. You will have to pay for the solicitor’s advice.
